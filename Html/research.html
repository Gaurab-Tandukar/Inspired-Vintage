PURPOSE OF ANALYSIS

In the competitive world of e-commerce, it is critical to understand what makes a platform special. Our website's components are analyzed and compared to those of five well-known platforms: XTRA Watch, Good Trouble, Titan Nepal, Door Dash etc. We will examine their features, services, and user interaction strategies to highlight the unique benefits of our website in offering a remarkable buying experience.We have tried our best to make it visually appealing as possible by taking reference from the sites mentioned below.

Home Page

  • We have profoundly tried to represent the website viewers with a design similar to XTRA WATCH.
